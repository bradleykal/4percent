The Perverse Incentive

A brilliant young doctor completing pediatric residency faces a choice: specialize in pediatric oncology for 3 more years of training at ~$65K salary, then earn $175K–$304K for life—OR switch to adult oncology for similar training time and earn $100K–$200K more annually. Over a 30-year career, that's $3–6 million in lost lifetime earnings. We are structurally discouraging the doctors our kids need most.
