Year 1: Discovery Before Technology

Before we build, we listen. Our entire first year is structured as a systematic discovery phase across three parallel tracks‚Äîunderstanding the ecosystem's needs before committing to any specific product or service.

Track 1

Academic & Clinical Institutions

  • Fellowship programs at MUSC, Dana-Farber, Texas Children's, St. Jude
  • Interview attending physicians on data needs and research gaps
  • Map compensation structures and training pipelines
  • Identify which institutions are most data-constrained
  • Explore LOI potential for physician loan programs
Track 2

Pharmaceutical Industry

  • Map pediatric oncology pipeline across major and specialty pharma
  • Interview R&D teams on PRV program utilization post-Give Kids a Chance Act
  • Assess willingness to fund sponsored research reports
  • Identify where data gaps slow pediatric trial design
  • Explore partnership on RACE Act compliance support
Track 3

Advocacy Organizations

  • Meet with St. Baldrick's, Children's Cancer Cause, Kids v. Cancer
  • Understand where policy intelligence is currently lacking
  • Map legislative tracking capabilities across the sector
  • Assess appetite for shared data infrastructure vs. proprietary tools
  • Identify complementary vs. overlapping roles
